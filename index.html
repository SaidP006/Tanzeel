📖 About Us

Nurturing Minds, Building Futures

Tanzeel Nursery & Primary School is a premier educational institution located in Kati District, Zanzibar. We provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating learning environment that fosters holistic development.

Our school is dedicated to academic excellence, Islamic moral values, and the development of well-rounded individuals.
