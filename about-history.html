Our History

The story of Tanzeel School – from a small nursery to a thriving educational institution.

📜 Our Journey

A Legacy of Excellence Since 2011

Tanzeel Nursery & Primary School was established in 2011 with a vision to provide quality education rooted in Islamic values. What started as a small nursery with just 15 students has grown into a thriving educational institution with over 450 students and 30 dedicated teachers.

Over the past 15 years, we have remained committed to our founding principles: academic excellence, moral integrity, and holistic development. Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, innovation, and a steadfast dedication to nurturing future generations.

Today, Tanzeel School is recognized as one of the leading educational institutions in Zanzibar, known for our commitment to quality education, Islamic values, and the holistic development of every child.
