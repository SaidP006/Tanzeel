🏫 School Overview

Excellence in Education Since 2011

Tanzeel Nursery & Primary School is a premier educational institution located in Kati District, Zanzibar. We are dedicated to providing quality education that nurtures the whole child – academically, spiritually, and socially.

Our school offers a comprehensive curriculum that combines the Tanzanian national curriculum with Islamic values and modern teaching methodologies. We believe in creating a supportive and stimulating environment where every child can thrive.
